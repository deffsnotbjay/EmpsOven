How It Works

When notifications are enabled, you'll receive an alert on your device whenever:

  • A new delivery order is assigned to you
  • An assignment is about to expire

This works even when you've closed the rider dashboard, as long as your browser supports push notifications.

If you denied notifications by mistake, you'll need to allow them in your browser settings for this site.
